about

ftp://con is an online antifascist hacker conference organized in response to defcon's decision to have dhs secretary mayorkas give a keynote presentation at defcon 25.

we are entirely volunteer organized and very ad hoc, so if you'd like to help out, get in touch! our email is organizers@ftpcon.com.

cfp

if you would like to give a presentation, lightning talk, or do a show and tell of some cool stuff you've been hacking on, email us at organizers@ftpcon.com and put [cfp] in the subject line.
