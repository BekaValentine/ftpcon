about

ftp://con is an antifascist hacker conference organized in response to defcon's decision to have dhs secretary mayorkas give a keynote presentation at defcon 25.

we are entirely volunteer organized and very ad hoc, so if you'd like to help out, get in touch! our email is organizers@ftpcon.com.
